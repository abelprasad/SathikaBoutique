# Sathika Boutique — Design System

## Brand Overview

**Sathika Boutique** is a premium fashion and lifestyle boutique focused on elegance, craftsmanship, and timeless beauty.  
The design language blends **deep red and antique gold** with modern minimalism to create an aesthetic, luxurious shopping experience.

**Brand Keywords:**  
Elegant · Aesthetic · Handcrafted · Premium · Warm · Feminine · Timeless

| -------------- | ----------- | ------- |
| Background     | Ivory       | #FAF8F4 |
| Text (Primary) | Charcoal    | #1C1C1C |
| Muted Text     | Warm Grey   | #6E6E6E |

### Usage Rules

- **Red** is used for branding, headlines, and key CTAs
- **Gold** is used for accents, dividers, icons, and borders
- **Ivory** replaces pure white for a softer, more premium feel
- Avoid overusing gold — subtlety = luxury
